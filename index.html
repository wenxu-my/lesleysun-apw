01 — Brand Introduction
A Brand Built
for Modern Women.

Lesley Sun is a contemporary Malaysian womenswear brand rooted in the belief that everyday dressing should feel effortless, elevated, and deeply personal. Each collection is crafted for real women living full, modern lives — designed to move seamlessly from morning meetings to evening gatherings.

Our Mission
To empower women through fashion that is timeless, wearable, and quietly confident. Pieces that feel as good as they look.
Our Vision
To become Southeast Asia's most loved contemporary womenswear brand — synonymous with refined everyday elegance.
"I wanted to create pieces that women reach for again and again — not because they're trendy, but because they feel exactly right."
— Lesley Sun, Founder

03 — Brand DNA
The Lesley Sun Aesthetic.

Four design principles that define every collection — and the reason women keep coming back.

Timeless Femininity
Clean silhouettes that honour the feminine form without constraint. From soft draping to structured tailoring — every piece transcends seasons and trends.
Elevated Essentials
Premium fabrics. Thoughtful construction. Our wardrobe staples are built to outlast fast fashion and become the most-worn pieces in any woman's closet.
Neutral Palette
A signature range of creams, taupes, soft whites, and earthy tones — colours that layer effortlessly, travel beautifully, and photograph in every setting.
Everyday Luxury
Refined but liveable. We believe luxury is not about occasion — it is about how a garment makes you feel the moment you put it on.
04 — Target Audience
She Knows Exactly
Who She Is.

The Lesley Sun woman is aged 28 to 55. She is educated, career-driven, and deeply intentional about how she presents herself. She invests in quality over quantity — and chooses pieces that work as hard as she does.

The Working Professional
Lawyers, consultants, founders, creatives — women who need a wardrobe that works across every context, from boardroom to brunch.
The Fashion-Conscious Minimalist
She curates, not accumulates. Every piece earns its place and tells a story. She is drawn to the brand's neutral palette and refined sensibility.
The Urban Southeast Asian
Based in KL, Singapore, Penang, and beyond — culturally fluid, globally influenced, and deeply proud of a regional identity done beautifully.
The Lifestyle-Led Shopper
Dressing for brunch, travel, board meetings, events, and everything in between. She spends across fashion, dining, wellness, and travel.
More Than Customers
We Have People
Who Show Up.

Our community isn't measured in numbers. It's measured in the women who clear their calendars for Lesley Sun events, bring their circles along, and return season after season — not to shop, but to belong.

They participate in launches, styling sessions, workshops and festive gatherings. They are the most powerful asset any retail space can have: women who are already engaged, already loyal, always showing up.

02 — Founder Story
The Woman
Behind the Brand.

Lesley Sun founded the brand with a singular conviction: that stylish dressing should never feel complicated. Drawing from her own experience as a working woman navigating boardrooms, brunches, and everything in between, she built a wardrobe language that spoke to women like her.

Since its founding, Lesley Sun has grown from a curated boutique into a recognised regional womenswear destination with a deeply loyal community across Malaysia and Singapore.
